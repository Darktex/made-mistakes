After being denied three times into the New Xbox Experience Preview Program, I finally got in last night when Microsoft let in the remaining batch of registered users. What a pleasant surprise it was turning on my Xbox 360 around 10pm EST to find that a console update was awaiting me.

The whole process went by pretty fast. Took a few minutes to download and then I was off. I spent about 15 minutes crafting my avatar and posing him just right for my Gamertag picture. The amount of choice is pretty good when compared to Nintendo’s Miis. You can’t position your facial features or change the sizes but you can dress your avatar…something the Miis lack.

The entire interface looks so much better and is way more responsive. I activated my Netflix account and gave streaming movies a go. Worked as advertised and looked on par with a DVD. I’ll have to add some HD content later and see how that looks.

Installed Gears of War 2 to my hard-drive to see how that whole process worked. Took about 5 minutes to copy over 6GB’s and then it was ready to launch off my hard-drive. I didn’t notice any load time gains, but man was my machine quiet. I probably won’t install games often since my 20GB hard-drive is tapped out of space, but it’s nice to know I have the option.

Very pleased with the update. Microsoft did a great job with it!
