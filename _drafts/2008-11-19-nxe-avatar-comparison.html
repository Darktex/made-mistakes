With the release of the New Xbox Experience, I spent some time trying to craft an avatar that resembled me. I don’t think I pulled it off to the same degree of perfection as I did with my Mii and Rock Band characters. It’s in the ballpark but not quite there yet. Maybe it has something to do with the beard selection…

What I Like

  • Creating gamerpics with your avatar. While it’s not quite the same as using my own upload graphics or photos, it’s better than nothing.
  • Overall quality and style of the avatars. Not too cartoony and not overly realistic.
  • Being able to save your favorite outfits making it easier to change between them.

What I Don’t Like

  • Facial hair looks flat and painted on. Why not add some depth to it like in Rock Band? It would look OK if I had a stubble but I don’t.
  • Need more clothing options. Microsoft announced that they’ll be releasing more options in the coming weeks. Cool. But what I’d like to see is the ability to customize the colors of each article of clothing and accessory. Again just copy what Rock Band did with their character creator.
