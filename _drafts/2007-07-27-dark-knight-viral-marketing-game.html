Yesterday evening I saw a post on AICN about a website that had a photo of Uncle Sam, but defaced in a manner to look like the Joker from the new Batman flick. It had a counter that was ticking down and some latitude/longitude coordinates for would be henchmen to meet up at and join the Joker’s crew.

Today when the counter hit 00:00:00, a pretty sweet ARG (alternate reality game) started up. What made this unique was the level of involvement in the real world. The website had people set out on a scavenger quest for clues that when relayed back to the website would help solve each puzzle. It all started about 10 minutes after the counter ran out, when a plane fly over the location from the website with the first puzzle’s answer. If you didn’t have a henchman feeding you that information you could check the surveillance page to see the happenings.



The game continues through numerous checkpoints with various riddles that could be solved by Googling some facts and such. One crazy puzzle had an audio file of both short and long laughs edited together. It was Morse code! For about 2 minutes I tried to figure it out and then gave up when I saw the solution posted on AICN.

If you go to WhySoSerious.com now you can view some images and see the teaser trailer for the Dark Knight movie. It’s just a bunch of voice-overs and the new bat logo, but still pretty cool to hear Ledger as the Joker.
