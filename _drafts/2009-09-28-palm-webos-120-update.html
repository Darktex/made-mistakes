Well this update has been lackluster. Nothing all that new to write home about.

  • Scrolling seems somewhat improved. If anything it’s probably due from the fresh reboot…
  • Can save images and files directly from the web browser now.
  • Can copy and paste text and images from web pages now. Cool if you can actually remember what combination of finger gymnastics to pull.
  • Web browser doesn’t do any of the fun geolocation stuff the iPhone can. They’re both WebKit based so I’d assume it’s possible…
  • App Catalog is still in beta but it lets you assign a credit card for payments now. Hoping to see an influx of solid apps this week…
  • Calendar app still blows and is slow. No agenda or year views…really Palm?
  • Task app is marginally better, but you still can’t make items repeatable or talk with Google tasks. For pushing this whole idea of synergy I don’t get why it doesn’t sync upâ€”when contacts, calendar, email, and messaging do.
  • Camera app saw no improvements from what I can tell. White balance and size options would have been nice. As would direct support for Flickr uploads.
  • Still no word on video recording or if an official app or update are coming.
  • No Flash support. I’m sure it’s coming. Not sure I care or will ever have a need for it.
