While sitting down to eat dinner at Pizza Plant tonight, I noticed something familiar on the paper place mats. Usually they're littered with local advertisements for dentists, quit smoking scams, and veins veins veins treatments.

Directly in the middle, to the right of the kids menu, was an ad for Allstate insurance. Typography done up in a cut paper style with a creepy hand adjusting the A in Allstate.

Hmmm, this looks oddly reminiscent of the work of Saul Bass I thought. You know, the dude who designed a ton of opening film titles for Alfred Hitchcock and iconic logos for AT&T, the Girl Scouts, and United Airlines.

Not exactly sure how they arrived at this solution, but it sort of works if mayhem is representing a heroin addict who totals your car, or decides to rob your house while you're out on a date with your wife. Because hey, "You're In Good Hands With Allstate®" and all…
