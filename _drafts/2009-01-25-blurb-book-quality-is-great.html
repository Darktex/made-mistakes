I’m really happy with how my first Blurb Book out. I opt’ed for the more expensive satin paper which gives the pages a nice texture and thickness to them. Next time I’m definitely paying extra for a hardcover with slip to better protect it. The only negative I’ve found with the book is that some of my photographs printed a tad on the dark side. I think it has something to do with how my monitor is calibrated. Images usually look brighter for me, so if I remember to take that into consideration next time I should be fine.
