Pretty much the only reason I created an About.me page was to get free customized business cards printed by Moo.

If you weren’t aware – About.me is a custom profile and personal analytics dashboard that lets you quickly build a personal page that points users to your content from around the web. Its straightforward WYSIWYG editor makes customizing the design, fonts, colors, and background image a breeze.

To take advantage of the free stack of business cards (technically it’s not free – shipping costs $5.50), just hit up the offers section in your About.me dashboard to place an order. You can import all your info and design elements or start from scratch and customize everything.
